In Eterno

This project was undertaken with the goal of assembling and preserving an archive of the artistic creations of my grandfather throughout his lifetime. He was a highly creative individual who wrote poetry, made drawings and created pictures using a typewriter. The significance of this project extends beyond personal sentiment, as it serves to safeguard and make accessible a cultural and historical legacy for future generations.

Assembling the archive was a challenging process. It involved collecting his works from various sources such as photographs, old notebooks and analog files. I then had to organize and classify the material in a logical and user-friendly manner. This required me to learn coding and various techniques for digitizing and preserving his work.

Despite the difficulties, I am proud of the final result. This archive now offers not just myself, but also anyone interested, the opportunity to explore and appreciate my grandfather's poetry, drawings, and other pieces. It provides a deeper insight into his character and artistry.

I am deeply grateful for the assistance provided by chatgpt during this project, without which the goal of creating this archive would not have been possible.

In conclusion, this project has been a labor of love and a way to connect with my grandfather in a new way. It serves as a lasting tribute to his talent and creativity, and I hope it will inspire others to pursue their own passions and dreams.

Timeline - How Does It Work

The timeline above is the a dynamic and interactive way to display and organize information. It allows the user to easily understand how many "events" happened in a specific year and when my grandfather has been more active in producing stuff.

Filters

The Filters above are the primary interactive element for navigating through a collection of works. It allows the user to filter and view specific subsets of the collection by clicking on different rectangles, each representing a different subject. Each rectangle has an associated subject, and when a rectangle is clicked, it calls a function that filters the elements of the collection based on that subject. The function then updates the visibility of the elements on the screen by adding or removing a class that controls the CSS styles. The user interacting with this filters, can navigate trough different subsets of the collection and exploring the works in a dynamic and engaging way.

Slider

The slider, made using the swiper library, is a user-friendly and interactive way to navigate through a collection of elements, such as images or text. The library allows developers to easily create a slider with a variety of customizable options, such as navigation arrows, pagination, and the ability to loop through the elements.

Slider and songs

The slider is dynamic, responding to the click on a certain element by modifying the structure of the carousel. In this example, when a CD is clicked, the slider transforms into a player that allows the user to listen to the songs on that CD through a list. Also the background of the element is used to mark the elapsed time of the song
